## 🎯 Project Vision
VÉNARO aims to redefine the online shopping experience by combining a **cinematic frontend** with a **robust, Shopify-style admin panel**. Every design and technical decision is guided by one question: *"Does this feel celebrity-level expensive?"*

---

## 🛠️ Main Components
1. **Frontend**: Luxury shop, product discovery, and user accounts.
2. **Admin Panel**: Complete inventory, order, and customer management system.
3. **Database**: A complex, 23-table normalized schema for high scalability.
